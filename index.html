So you wanna know how to build a keyboard?
Building a custom mechanical keyboard can be quite a hassle if you're just starting out on your journey. That's why this site was made - to help newcomers understand what pieces they need to make the perfect keyboard just for them.

Keyboard components

A case is basically a box that holds all of the components in place.

A pcb is a plate which the switches connect into and then sends the signal to the pc

Switches are the buttons that represent each letter, they can either be linear, tactile or clicky.

Keycaps are the easiest way to change the look of the whole keyboard, usually made of either pbt or abs plastic.
